My research spans machine learning, clinical psychology, and statistical methodology, with a focus on (1) digital phenotyping and smartphone-based assessment of suicide risk and (2) regularization methods for structural equation modeling. For the complete and most current publication list, see my [Google Scholar profile](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=K7_cclwAAAAJ&hl=en).
